FAQ

What are Roman numerals in progressions?

They represent chord function in a key (I = tonic, V = dominant, etc.). That is why the same pattern can be reused in any key.

Do I need music theory to use this?

No. You can browse by mood or genre. Roman numerals are optional, but helpful if you want to understand the why.

Why playback a progression?

It lets you hear the progression instantly in the chosen key and check if it fits the song or exercise you are working on.
