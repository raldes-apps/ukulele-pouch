FAQ

Why does one chord have multiple fingerings?

The same chord can be played in different positions. Alternate shapes can make transitions easier or fit the sound you want.

Can the app play the chord sound?

Yes. Playback helps you confirm the sound quickly and is useful for learning and arranging.

Does playback work for every fingering of a chord?

Yes. Playback is available for all fingerings listed for that chord.

What if I cannot find a rare chord?

Many unusual chords can be identified by entering the fingering on the interactive fretboard.
