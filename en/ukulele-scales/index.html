Scales are not just running notes. When you see them directly on the fretboard, you understand note locations faster and how chords are built from those notes.

Ukulele Pouch highlights scale tones on the fretboard and shows diatonic chords - chords that naturally belong to that scale.

FAQ

What are diatonic chords?

Chords built only from the notes of the scale. They show which chords naturally belong together in a key.

Is this beginner-friendly?

Yes. Seeing notes on the fretboard is often easier than learning from theory alone.

Why play scales and chords?

Playback helps you verify you are practicing the right notes and quickly hear the scale character and harmony.
