Ukulele metronome (Tap Tempo)

A metronome is the foundation of solid rhythm. In Ukulele Pouch you can set an exact tempo or tap it in quickly using Tap Tempo.

The mini player keeps the metronome accessible while browsing chords, progressions, or scales - no need to jump between screens.

Who it is for / when it helps

  • When you tend to speed up or slow down without noticing
  • When you need to find a song's tempo quickly with Tap Tempo
  • When you practice strumming patterns and want them locked in time
  • When you drill chord transitions and do not want to chase the tempo
  • When you are increasing BPM gradually and want controlled speed-ups
  • When you warm up before playing and want steady tempo from the start
  • When you play with someone else and need a shared tempo

Tips

  • Start slower than you think, then speed up only when it is clean
  • Loop two chords for 1-2 minutes to build stable transitions

FAQ

What is Tap Tempo?

You tap along with a beat and the app calculates the BPM from your taps. It is faster than entering numbers.

Why have a metronome mini player?

So you can control the metronome while using other tools like chords, progressions, or scales.

Why use a metronome for simple songs?

It improves timing and keeps your tempo steady. Even simple strumming sounds better when rhythm is consistent.
