Floating tools button

Do not want to keep digging for tuner and metronome in menus? The floating tools button gives you one-tap access to key tools from anywhere.

It is optional: you can enable or disable it and move it to a spot where it will not get in the way.

Key benefits

  • One-tap access to tools from anywhere
  • Quick access to tuner, metronome, and more
  • Optional - enable or disable anytime
  • Movable position on the screen
  • Saves time when switching tools

Who it is for / when it helps

  • You often switch between the tuner, metronome, chords, and PDFs
  • You want tools available without digging through menus
  • During lessons you need to show a chord or start the metronome quickly
  • You switch tools repeatedly between songs or during rehearsal

Tips

  • Place it where it does not cover PDF text
  • If it feels distracting, disable it and rely on the metronome mini player

FAQ

Can I disable the floating button?

Yes. It is optional, so you can keep the UI clean if you prefer.

Why is it movable?

To avoid covering content, especially when reading PDFs or selecting chords.

When is it most useful?

During drills or teaching: you can switch tools instantly without breaking your rhythm.
